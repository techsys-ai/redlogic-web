Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2026

RedLogic.io ("RedLogic," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our managed software platform services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Client business information: Business name, contact name, email address, phone number, and billing information provided during onboarding
  • Platform usage data: Admin activity logs, login timestamps, and feature usage for security and support purposes
  • End-customer data: Data entered by our Clients about their own customers — stored on their behalf as part of the managed platform service
  • Website inquiry data: Name, email, and message content submitted through our contact form

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provision, operate, and maintain the platforms we manage on Client behalf
  • Process billing and payments
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Send service-related communications including invoices and maintenance notices
  • Improve our services and platform capabilities

3. End-Customer Data

RedLogic acts as a data processor for end-customer data entered by our Clients. Our Clients are the data controllers for their customers' information. We do not access, use, or analyze end-customer data except as necessary to provide the platform services. We do not sell end-customer data under any circumstances.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Hosting infrastructure providers (DigitalOcean) and payment processors, bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (SSL/TLS), hashed password storage, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We notify affected Clients promptly in the event of a data breach.

6. Data Retention

We retain Client and platform data for the duration of the service relationship plus 30 days following termination, during which time Clients may export their data. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Cookies

Our marketing website (redlogic.io) uses only essential session cookies for form functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics on our marketing site.

8. Your Rights

Clients may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their business information at any time by contacting us at hello@redlogic.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify Clients of material changes via email. Continued use of our services after notice constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: hello@redlogic.io  ·  Houston, TX
