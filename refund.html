Refund Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all managed software platform services provided by RedLogic.io. We want our clients to be satisfied with their platforms, and we stand behind the quality of our work.

1. Monthly Service Fees

All monthly licensing and managed service fees are billed in advance and are generally non-refundable. Because our infrastructure, hosting, and support costs are incurred at the start of each billing period, we are unable to offer pro-rated refunds for partial months.

Standard Policy

Monthly service fees are non-refundable once the billing period has begun. Clients may cancel at any time with 30 days written notice and will not be billed for the following month.

2. Setup & Onboarding Fees

One-time setup and onboarding fees are non-refundable once platform deployment has begun. If RedLogic is unable to deliver a functional platform as described in the service agreement, a full refund of the setup fee will be issued.

3. Exceptions — When Refunds Are Issued

RedLogic will issue refunds in the following circumstances:

  • Service failure: If the platform experiences downtime exceeding 72 consecutive hours due to causes within RedLogic's control, a pro-rated credit will be applied to the following invoice
  • Billing error: If a Client is charged incorrectly, the overcharge will be refunded within 5 business days of confirmation
  • Deployment failure: If RedLogic is unable to deploy a functional platform as agreed, setup fees will be refunded in full
  • Cancellation within 7 days: New Clients who cancel within 7 days of their first invoice, before actively using the platform, may request a full refund of their first month's fee

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at hello@redlogic.io with your business name, invoice number, and reason for the request. We will review and respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to the original payment method.

5. Cancellation

Clients may cancel their service at any time by providing 30 days written notice to hello@redlogic.io. Upon cancellation:

  • Service continues through the end of the current billing period
  • No further invoices will be issued after the cancellation date
  • Client data is available for export for 30 days following service termination
  • No refund is issued for the remaining days in the current billing period

6. Disputes

If you believe you have been charged in error or have a billing dispute, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Most issues can be resolved quickly and directly. Chargebacks that are found to be invalid may result in service suspension and additional fees.

7. Contact

Refund requests and billing questions: hello@redlogic.io  ·  Houston, TX
